## Using Models

Models are used in the following endpoints:

- **Text-to-Speech Generation**: `POST /text-to-speech/:model_id` (with `streaming: false` for WAV file)
- **Text-to-Speech Streaming**: `POST /text-to-speech/:model_id` (with `streaming: true` for PCM16 stream)
- **WebSocket Streaming**: `WSS /text-to-speech` (with `model_id` in initConnection)

<Note>
  **Model Selection**: Choose Faseeh v1 Preview for general use cases requiring high quality, or Faseeh v1 Turbo Preview for applications requiring low latency and faster generation.
</Note>
